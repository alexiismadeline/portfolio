Alexis Madeline

Contact / Bio

Alexis Madeline is a multidisciplinary artist based in NYC. Her work reflects the dynamic relationship between art and technology, evoking fascination and apprehension. By studying the malleability of time, she captures life's rawness, focusing on its impermanence. By utilizing the moving image to depict her subjects in a state of life, she invites viewers to contemplate the fluidity of reality and the complexities of the human experience.
